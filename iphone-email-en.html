Set Up Email on an iPhone

Back to Knowledge Base

Steps

  1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
  2. Scroll down and tap **Mail**.
  3. Select **Accounts** and then tap **Add Account**.
  4. Choose your email provider (e.g., Gmail or Outlook). If itâ€™s not listed, choose **Other**.
  5. Enter your email address, password, and name, then tap **Next**.
  6. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.

Your email has now been added to your iPhone!

Set Up Email on an Android Device

Read another manual

Steps

  1. Open the Settings app on your Android device.
  2. Go to Accounts or Accounts & Sync.
  3. Tap Add Account and select Email or your specific provider.
  4. Enter your email address and password, then tap Next.
  5. If required, configure the server settings (ask your provider for details).
  6. Complete the steps and tap Save.

Your email is now set up on your Android device!
